Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2025

Overview

Pilotik is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data you create in Pilotik — routines, moves, class history, scheduled classes, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data stays on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No location data
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data shared with third parties

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud on your device, your Pilotik data may sync across your Apple devices via Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Pilotik does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Pilotik does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at pilotikapp@gmail.com.
